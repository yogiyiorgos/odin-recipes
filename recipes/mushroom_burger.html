Mushroom Burger

Description

Ingredients

  • Cheakpeas
  • Mushrooms
  • Flaxseeds
  • Bread crumbs
  • Oats
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Combine the flaxseeds with the water
  2. Mash the chickpeas with a fork
  3. Put 1/2 cup of oats into a blender
  4. Finely chop the onion
  5. Dice the mushrooms
  6. Heat some oil in a small pan
  7. Add all the remaining ingredients in the mix
  8. Form four large burger patties
