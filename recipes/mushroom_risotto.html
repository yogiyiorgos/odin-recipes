Mushroom Risotto

Description

Ingredients

  • Risotto rice
  • Dried mushrooms
  • White wine
  • Vegetable broth
  • Nutritional yeast
  • White miso paste
  • Almond butter

Steps

  1. Slice the mushrooms
  2. Soak the dried mushrooms
  3. Finely chop the onion
  4. Drain the dried mushrooms
  5. Heat some oil in a large pan
  6. Add the mushrooms
  7. Add the rice and cook
  8. Deglaze the white wine
  9. Make the roasted dried tomatoes
  10. Stir the nutritional yeast
  11. Sprinkle with freshly chopped parsley
